Our Mission

Support for Orphaned Children:
Ilangu Trust is committed to bringing joy, hope, and opportunities to orphaned children by providing them with care, education, and support to help them thrive.
Awareness Among B.Ed. Students:
We focus on educating B.Ed. college students, equipping them to recognize and address drug addiction challenges among their future students.
Interactive School Programs:
Through interactive classes in schools, we educate young minds about the dangers of substance abuse, promoting a drug-free future for our communities.

OUR VISION

Empowered Orphaned Children:
Our vision is to create a world where every orphaned child can smile with confidence, live with dignity, and achieve their dreams.
Informed Educators and Students:
We envision an empowered society where educators and students are well-informed about the risks of drug addiction.
Building Stronger Communities:
Together, we aim to work towards building healthier, safer, and more compassionate communities for generations to come.

Who are We .....

  • Mission: Ilangu Trust aims to shape a better future by inspiring individuals to become the change they wish to see in the world.
  • Symbol of "I": The "I" in ILANGU represents personal responsibility and action, emphasizing that change starts with each individual.
  • Commitment: The organization is dedicated to creating an environment where children can learn about the dangers of drug abuse and the importance of value-based education.
  • Goal: The ultimate objective is to guide youth toward holistic development by engaging them in meaningful activities and promoting positive thinking, leading to prosperity.
  • Empowerment of Future Leaders: Ilangu Trust works to empower future leaders and responsible citizens who can contribute positively to society.
  • Impact: Through various efforts, we bring joy to orphaned children, raise awareness among educators, and educate young minds about the dangers of substance abuse.
  • Vision: We strive to empower individuals to take the lead in creating a brighter, healthier, and more compassionate future.
  • Core Belief: At ILANGU, we believe in the principle that "I must be the change to change the future."
