SMILE

Project SMILE is an initiative designed to bring joy to orphaned children by engaging them in creative activities such as crafts and artwork. This event not only focuses on fostering smiles and positive experiences for the children, but also emphasizes addressing their essential survival needs. Through this initiative, we aim to create a moment of joy and emotional support, while ensuring that the children’s basic needs are met, contributing to their well-being and happiness..

ILAKKU

ILANGU NGO is committed to creating awareness within communities by actively involving college volunteer students. Through various topics and discussions focused on the needs of the community, these volunteers help facilitate meaningful conversations that educate and inspire action. By leveraging the energy and enthusiasm of the students, ILANGU NGO aims to address critical issues, promote understanding, and encourage community engagement, ultimately fostering a collaborative effort to meet the needs of vulnerable populations

IYANGU

ILANGU NGO organized an impactful Awareness Program for MSW students at Women’s Christian College (WCC), Chennai, aimed at enhancing their understanding of key social issues and the role they play in addressing them. The program focused on equipping students with valuable insights and skills for their future careers in social work. In recognition of their commitment and excellence, participants were awarded certificates to acknowledge their dedication and progress. This initiative not only reinforced their career paths but also inspired them to continue striving for positive social change in their professional journey.

ILAKKU

In the month of March, we take a moment to extend our heartfelt appreciation to all social workers for their unwavering dedication and tireless efforts throughout their journey. Their commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of individuals and communities is truly commendable. Social workers consistently demonstrate compassion, resilience, and a deep sense of responsibility in addressing the needs of vulnerable populations. ILANGU NGO recognizes and honors their invaluable contributions and the significant difference they make in creating a better, more inclusive society.
